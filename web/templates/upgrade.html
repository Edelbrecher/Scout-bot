❓ Common Questions
Can I try before subscribing? ▾

Yes — new guilds get a free trial period to explore all features. If your trial has expired, upgrading takes less than 60 seconds via Stripe. You can cancel any time from your billing page.

What's the difference between Player Pro and Alliance plans? ▾

Player Pro ($2.99) is for individual players — no Discord server needed. It unlocks personal analytics like Farming Intelligence and your own operation plan.

Alliance plans (from $6.99) include everything in Player Pro plus server-wide features: Sector Monitor, operation planning, alliance tracking, defense coordination, and more.

Can I change my plan later? ▾

Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from the billing page. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the end of your billing period.

How does the map scanning work? ▾

TravOps fetches a full map snapshot from your Travian world every 15 minutes. Each snapshot captures all village populations and coordinates. By comparing snapshots over time, we can detect inactive farms (stable population), track growth, and alert you to troop movements in monitored sectors.
